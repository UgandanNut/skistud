Hello, I’m Ethan Pszanowski

Ski Studios, founded in 2023 by Ethan Pszanowski, is a premier audio engineering service dedicated to the art of capturing, refining, and perfecting the sound of music. We provide our artists with the tools and experience necessary to truly capture their vision. At Ski Studios, we understand that each artist has a unique vision for their music. That's why we pride ourselves on working diligently with our clients. Equipped with industry-standard equipment and software, Ski Studios leaves no stone unturned when it comes to delivering uncompromising audio quality. From pressing records to bouncing the master, we pay careful attention to every detail throughout the recording, editing, mixing, and mastering processes. With a dedication to excellence and a passion for the craft, Ski Studios is the destination for artists looking to unleash the full potential of their music and make a lasting impact in the industry. My Services

Audio Recording

Great recordings are essential for a great mix. Record at the TRCOA Campus Stinson Studios using top line equipment in expert designed rooms!

Audio Editing

Editing is the first step to a perfect mix! Clip gaining ensures everything is at a good level going in, pitch adjustment cleans up the vocals, and time alightment gets your song feeling tight.

Mixing

The mixing phases brings your song to life. EQ and compression clean up your recordings, while effects like delays and reverbs breath life into the mix! This is where your song gets its sound.

Mastering

Mastering is the icing on the cake! A little EQ here, a little compression here, and a nice volume boost to top it off, ensuring your mix is radio ready!
